The 82nd Air Defense Jet Regiment was established at Nasosni Air Base on March 1, 1940. The 82nd Air Defense Jet Regiment was established in the USSR Air Force and participated in the Second World War. In May 1942, passed on to the Hawker Hurricane plane, which was sent to the USSR on loan. Yak-9 aircraft were used from 1944 to 1952. After the war, they were deployed at the military airport Nasosniya and were tasked with protecting the Baku and Baku oil fields. 82nd Air Defense Hunting Regiment used MiG-15, MiG-17, Yak-28P and MiG-25 aircraft. After the collapse of the USSR, it came under the protection of the Azerbaijan Republic.
